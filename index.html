About Me

Hi there! Some things you should know about me are that I love to travel, am a DIY project aficionado, love IKEA (so much), have spent a few years playing competitive dodgeball, genuinely love helping people, find myself always learning something about something, enjoy a good documentary over many movies, and I am insanely curious about how stuff works. When I find a problem, I can’t rest until I understand what the dealio is, or even better, if I’ve got a solution for it.

I believe that there is always a way to solution for anything, even if that is not what you were originally thinking it would be. Here’s to hoping that helps with the coding and then eventually leads me down a very exciting and very technical path. Coding has been one of my bucket list items for the past few years, and I’m so excited to finally be learning!
